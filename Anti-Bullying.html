Stand Against Bullying

What is Bullying?

Bullying is repeated aggressive behavior intended to hurt another person, physically, emotionally, or socially. Cyberbullying is when this behavior takes place online through social media, messages, or digital platforms.

Why Prevention Matters

Bullying can have serious effects on a personâ€™s mental health, self-esteem, and overall well-being. Preventing it means creating safe spaces, teaching respect, and encouraging kindness both offline and online.

How You Can Help

  • Speak up when you see bullying.
  • Support victims and let them know they are not alone.
  • Use social media responsibly.
  • Promote kindness and respect in your community.
